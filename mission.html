At 𝐙𝐎𝐓𝐀𝐂𝐓𝐘𝐒𝐎𝐍- सम्राज्य 🧃, we help users enjoy social media safely, responsibly, and creatively. Learn to protect yourself and your content while creating a positive online experience.

🛡️Protect Your Personal Information

Do not share sensitive data online. Keep your profile private when necessary.

🔒Use Strong Passwords & 2FA

Create complex passwords and enable two-factor authentication to secure your account.

📝Think Before You Post

Remember online content can be permanent. Avoid sharing sensitive or harmful content.

⚠️Be Careful with Messages & Links

Do not click on suspicious links or accept messages from unknown users.

How to Report Unsafe Accounts

  • Open the user profile or post → Tap the three dots (⋮) → Select Report → Choose reason.
  • Block users to prevent contact.
  • Report impersonation if someone pretends to be you or others.
  • For illegal activity, report to the platform and local authorities.

Filing a Legal Complaint

  • Document all evidence: screenshots, messages, and dates.
  • Contact local law enforcement or cybercrime authorities.
  • File online complaints in India at Cyber Crime Reporting Portal.
  • Consult cyber law professionals for serious cases.
